Privacy Policy

VHS ("we", "our", or "us") built the QR Scanner app as a free application. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection and Use

QR Scanner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data processed by the app remains on your device.

Camera Permission

The app requires access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is not recorded, stored, or shared with any third party.

Data Storage

Scan history and app settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
